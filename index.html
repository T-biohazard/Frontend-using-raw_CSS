Tour management involves coordinating and overseeing the logistical aspects of a tour for artists or performers. This includes planning transportation, accommodations, and scheduling for the entire duration of the tour. The tour manager is responsible for ensuring that the travel arrangements run smoothly, resolving any issues that may arise, and communicating effectively with the tour crew and performers.
